Parents Section

Admission Process

Admission Requirements

  1. 2 colored passport size photos for the child
  2. A copy of the pupil’s birth certificate and immunization card
  3. A copy of parent(s)/ Guardian(s) National Identity (ID) card or passport
  4. Full school uniform (to be purchased at the school).

Filing an Application after Admissions Decision

This is the process where you file and sign the school admission form.

Admission fee is ksh 2,000

Deposit

This is the process where you pay school fees of the child being admitted.




NB: Details of fee payment is in the next section

Matriculation

This is the final stage in the admission process, where the child reports to school




NB: As a student of Tahillah Preparatory School.
